Master of Data Science at Monash University and Bachelor of Industrial Engineering and Science at Universidad del Valle de Guatemala. I often get the question, why did I choose to study Data Science, and what exactly is Data Science?

To answer that question, my work experience relies on multinational companies such as Colgate and CBC (PepsiCo’s bottling company) which on a daily basis need to analyze large datasets in order to make the best decisions. I always had a feeling that it would be possible to achieve more with the data aside from the traditional Excel spreadsheets, this led me to pursue a career in Data Science. I chose Monash University in Australia to study my masters as it is an exceptional university placed in the Group of 8, meaning it is one of the top universities.

Throughout my career, I have used tools such as Python, SQL, R, Tableau, and Linux shell to explore, analyze and give meaning to data. My career is heavily oriented to Data Wrangling, Data Processing, Data Analysis, Statistical Analysis, and especially importantly Data Visualization. After finishing my degree, I am confident to say I choose the right path as the possibilities are endless and each day I push the limits of what can be done with data. The topic that excites me the most is Machine Learning and Deep Learning. Just to give you a quick glimpse, the image at the top of this webpage is a picture of me on one of my many trips throughout Australia. A Deep Learning technique called Style Transfer was applied which captures the patterns of a particular painting and applies it to my photograph. It’s art, just a new and different style of art.

In my free time, you’ll see me playing baseball or going for a run. Life should be balanced so on weekends you can find me exploring new neighborhoods, restaurants, or anywhere in Australia. Time goes by fast when you’re busy trying to plan a journey, so get out there and start doing it.
